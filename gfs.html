Grants for startups

Looking for a way to fund your next great idea or enhance your skills and knowledge in the business and start-up world? 

Here is the list of the companies and foundations in Armenia that implement acceleration and incubation programs, support innovators and provide funding for startups.

Armenian Accelerator and Incubator companies

1.Enterprise Incubator Foundation (EIF)

Enterprise Incubator Foundation (EIF) is one of the largest technology business incubators and consulting companies in the region, operating in Yerevan, Armenia. EIF’s activities cover every aspect of sector development –ICT-related legal, business and educational reforms, investment channeling and creation of funding schemes for startups, individualized services and consulting for IT companies, talent identification and workforce development.

https://www.eif.am/

2.Innovative Solutions and Technologies Center (ISTC)

ISTC Foundation is one of the leading innovation centers in Armenia, founded by joint initiative of IBM, USAID, Armenian Government and Enterprise Incubator Foundation since 2015. Legally entity was registered in 2018. The main goal of the ISTC is to develop and strengthen the educational capabilities of Armenian higher educational institutions in High-Tech, to enhance their research potential, as well as creating a product development environment for formation of sustainable tech businesses.

https://istc.am/

3.Microsoft Innovation Center Armenia

Microsoft Innovation Center Armenia jointly established by United States Agency for International Development (USAID), Microsoft Corporation and EIF in 2011, now joins the global network of innovation expansion and development in IT. MIC Armenia initiates various targeted projects and activities, which include training programs and coding bootcamps, aimed at development of qualified workforce, programs, targeted at teams and startups to support the development of innovative IT products in various spheres.

https://www.mic.am/

4.Granatus Ventures

Granatus Ventures is the first Venture Capital firm in Armenia to provide funding, expertise and networks to promising technology-driven startups based in, or having core value-add activities in Armenia. The principal activity of the Fund is investing in a range of IP, technology or innovation-based companies that capitalize on opportunities in high-growth sectors including but not limited to: ICT (enterprise software, consumer internet, digital media, mobile technologies); engineering (hardware, software and electronics); materials sciences and clean-tech. The Fund’s primary focus is on seed, start-up and early-stage privately held companies. 

https://www.granatusventures.com/

5.Hero House

Hero House is an innovation hub in Yerevan, Armenia. It’s goal is to enable more heroes for spreading freedom, progress and change in Armenia. Hero House is in the heart of Yerevan startup ecosystem. Hero House is running a series of online talks and panel discussions to leverage the deep knowledge of industry professionals and bring international best practices into the entrepreneurial community shaped around Hero House and beyond.

https://www.herohouse.am/

6.Armenia Startup Academy

Armenia Startup Academy is a game-changing entrepreneurship and startup education program, which aims to prepare startups to raise their pre-seed rounds and expand to global markets through its Pre-acceleration and Traction programs. Startups can be in different fields: AI / ML, I օ T, Healthtech, Traveltech և etc. The program lasts 12 weeks.

https://www.startupacademy.am/

7.Entrepreneurship and Product Innovation Center (EPIC)

EPIC is the American University of Armenia's (AUA) on-campus start-up incubator that helps AUA students, alumni, and other entrepreneurs to advance their ventures from idea to success. EPIC program is implemented twice a year in the form of spring and autumn programs. The spring season starts in February, and the autumn season starts in September. Within the framework of the program, a 12-week program is planned, which involves special lectures with the best specialists in the field.

https://www.epic.aua.am/

8.ImpactAim

ImpactAim is the first accelerator in Armenia designed to drive the scale up of local and international impact ventures. We support start-ups and innovators by providing expertise, access to professional networks, funding and universal field reach.

https://www.impactaim.com/

9.Beeline Startup Incubator

Beeline Startup Incubator started its activity in 2018. It is a platform, where startups with a great potential of success will be educated by industry leaders and communicate with successful entrepreneurs. Currently Beeline Startup Incubator does not accept idea-stage startups. Startups with MVP or traction can participate in the program.

https://www.incubator.beeline.am/ 

10.Founder Institute

The accelerator of the Founder Institute of Yerevan branch (which opened in Armenia in 2017) is an accelerator program for startups in the world’s leading pre-seed stage to develop people’s ideas. The Founder Institute’s mission is to “Globalize Silicon Valley” and create sustainable startup ecosystems that will create one million new jobs worldwide.

https://www.fi.co/s/yerevan

11.HIVE Ventures

HIVE Ventures was founded in 2014 with the mission to catalyze the global Armenian tech ecosystem by investing in startups led by Armenian entrepreneurs around the world. We not only provide venture capital but also offer mentorship and a Silicon Valley network of leading founders, operators and VCs. HIVE Ventures is a Hirair and Anna Hovnanian Foundation Initiative. 

https://www.hiveventures.co/

12.Catalyst Foundation

Catalyst Foundation is established by the founding partners of SmartGateVC for running game-changing programs which will feed the Armenian startup ecosystem with relevant skillset and pipeline of capable companies. Catalyst Foundation's mission is to initiate and support projects that can have a significant impact on generation of pipeline of tech companies capable of global expansion.

https://www.catalyst.am/

13.Foundation for Armenian Science and Technology (FAST)

FAST is building an ecosystem of innovation to lead scientists, technologists, and innovators in Armenia and beyond to success on the global stage.

FAST has two main business development programs. The first is an incubation program that supports existing startups. During the incubation program the participants are provided with co-working space, various courses, educational and project modules, etc. With a focus on entrepreneurial endeavors, FAST empowers innovators to bring cutting-edge, commercially viable, and globally competitive solutions to life.

https://www.fast.foundation/

Russian Accelerator and Incubation Programs

1.b8

B8 accelerator began to work in  2019 in Russia, Yakutsk. B8 is a program of accelerated development for startups, which includes mentoring, infrastructure, educational and financial support up to 2 million rubles for the development of the project and the team. B8 gives an opportunity to pitch your project to potential investors. Program duration is 3 months. 

https://www.b8accelerator.com/

2.Pulsar VC

The acceleration program's participants cooperate with experts, mentors and investors in Kazan, Innopolis and Dublin (Ireland). The participating startups will receive investments up to 8,000,000 rubles from the accelerator and the organizers of the program. The organizers claim the goal of the accelerator to be the access of domestic technology players into the international market.

https://www.accelerator.pulsar.vc/

3.Russol

Russol  is an online startup school which helps the participants of the acceleration to pump up their knowledge and introduce them to the business experience of successful entrepreneurs from trending industries. To attend lectures and get expert leveling, participants do not need to travel distances. Everything is online. There are no legal barriers. Participants do not charge for viewing video recordings of lectures, notes, transcripts and for the participation of startups in the program.

https://www.russol.info/

4.Internet Initiatives Development Fund (IIDF)

The accelerator specializes in startups in Cybersecurity, Retail, Adtech, Edutech, Big Data and IOT. Over 4,500 startups participate in the basic online program every year, with 20,000 attending events and hackathons.

https://www.iidf.vc/

American Accelerator and Incubator Companies

1.Y Combinato

Y Combinator is a pioneer in the startup accelerator space. Each year the accelerator funds a group of new startups with $120k. A number that was lowered to reduce friction between founders. So far, the companies it has been involved with have a combined valuation of over $100B. Some of the most notable include: Airbnb, Dropbox, Stripe, Reddit, Twitch, Coinbase, and Weebly.

https://www.ycombinator.com/

2.500 Startups

500 Startups is a seed and early stage venture capital fund, consisting of 4 major funds and 13 micro funds which have invested in startups in at least 60 countries. Funded startups include Udemy and Credit Karma. Exits have included sales to Google and Rakuten. 500 Startups recently took in equity from Abu Dhabi Financial Group, giving the firm one of its only two board seats.

https://www.500.co/

3.TechStars

Techstars funds, mentors and accelerates startups. Its accelerator program has produced over 1,000 companies valued at over $8B. Techstars is the name behind Startup Week and Startup Weekend, which spur entrepreneurs to kick procrastination to the curb and  launch new ventures in a matter of hours.

https://www.techstars.com/
